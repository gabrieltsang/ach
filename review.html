What you should know:
  • Adding an additional cardholder is free with a maxium of 4 people added to the card.
  • Primary cardholder will share existing credit limit and available credit.
  • Additional cardholder must have 16 years old.
  • The application will be saved automatically.
  • You can retrieve your application with the reference code sent to your email address.
